Learn More About Bespoke Jewelry

What is "bespoke" jewelry?

Anything “bespoke” is custom-made or made-to-order. It's anything that has been specifically designed with the customer in mind and in direct collaboration with that customer. A jeweler that offers bespoke, or custom-made, jewelry can sit down with a customer and work with them to create a piece that is one of a kind!

Who am I?

My name is Sarah and for the past twenty years I have been providing the Boise area with the finest, quickest and most affordable custom-made jewelry while still providing a personal touch. I opened this store back in 2001 with my husband, Jerry, when he had a hard time finding an engagement ring at stores around us. Luckily the bespoke jewelry business was taking off at the time and we have been here ever since!

What is this website for?

Following my blog posts allows you to get an inside look at what goes into making jewelry, as well as, getting insider scoops on the latest deals and store information. My blog should be updated at least once a week with whatever piece I have been working on that week. Join me on my journey and meet interesting people and here fun stories!
